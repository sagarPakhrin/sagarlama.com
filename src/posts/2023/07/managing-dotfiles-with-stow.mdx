### What are dotfiles?

Dotfiles are plain text configuration files for most of the applications of unix-like systems. 
These files begin with a dot(`.`) and are hidden by default. The preferences and configurations you'ved made gets saved in the dotfiles. If you run `ls -a ~/` you will see a list of dotfiles in your home directory.
In this blog post we will learn how to backup and seamlessly replicate your configurations using git and GNU Stow.

### Why backup dotfiles?

Wether you want to replicate your configurations on multiple machines, you want to share your config with others, or you just want to backup your config so it doesn't get lost,
no matter the reason, you can still manage your dotfiles with git.

Symbolic Link also known as symlink is simply a shortcut to another file. It is a file that points to another file.
